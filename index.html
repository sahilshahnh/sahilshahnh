About Me


Hi there! I'm currently a senior at Nashua High School South in New Hampshire. My interests include tennis, reading, traveling, graphic design, and 3D computer aided design and modeling. At the moment I mostly keep busy with academics, sports, math and science research at UMass Lowell, and various volunteer involvements.


I love reading, and enjoy literature ranging from school-assigned classics to fantasy novels (currently, favorite book is probably "The Curious Incident of the Dog in the Night-Time")

I enjoy playing a wide variety of sports like football and ping-pong, but tennis is my favorite.

My family and I travel frequently, and have visited more than 30 countries in 5 continents!

Despite my Indian heritage, Indian cuisine is one of my least favorite (I love Mexican though!).

I am very unpredictable when it comes to music taste, listening to rap one day and classical the next.
